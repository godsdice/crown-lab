Cold Spring Harbor Germ Cells Meeting

October 11, 2018

Nicole presented a talk at the Cold Spring Harbor Germ Cells Meeting! This is my favorite meeting as it’s broadly focused on germ cell biology. As molecular geneticists, we frequently hyper-focus on what’s happening in the nucleus and forget that meiosis is happening within the context of development!

Nicole Crown: interchromosomal effect of crossover formation. If crossover is suppressed on one chromosome pair due to inversions, other chromosome increase crossovers. #cshlgermcells

Really cool work by @nicolecrown2 on how balancers affect crossover frequency on other non-balanced chromosomes #cshlgermcells
